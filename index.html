Surfing in the Bay Area

A Newcomer's Guide to Surfing

Don't Be Scared

When most people think of surfing in NorCal, they think of cold water, huge waves, and Great White Sharks. However, the summertime brings warmer water, calmer surf and less sharks. It is the perfect time for a beginner, or a SoCal surfer that is used to warmer water and smaller waves to warm up to surfing in the Bay.

Rockaway Beach, Pacifica
 

Gear

If you want to surf, you are definetly going to need some gear. Unless you are an experienced surfer, I would not reccomend spending too much money. You can usually find used gear for cheap.
